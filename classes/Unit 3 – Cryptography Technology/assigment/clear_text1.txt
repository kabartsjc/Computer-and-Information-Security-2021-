Traditionally, haiku poems are three-line stanzas with a 5/7/5 syllable count. 
This form of poetry also focuses on the beauty and simplicity found in nature. 
As its popularity grew, the 5/7/5 formula has often been broken. 
However, the focus remains the same - simple moments in life.
